Born and raised in the 'Show-Me' state, I moved to Memphis shortly after graduating from college for what I thought would be a 10-week summer job repairing homes in Binghampton. Ten years later I'm still here and love being a Memphian. In that time I've met and married my wife, adopted two pups, bought a home, made lifelong friends, and watched as this city has changed before my eyes. I've had the honor of working with a few of Memphis' most reputable organizations and take pride in being an active member in shaping my community.

WORK HISTORY

MEMPHIS REDBIRDS / 901 FC - Memphis,TN

DIRECTOR OF FIELD OPERATIONS

June 2018-Present

ASSISTANT GROUNDSKEEPER

March 2014-June 2018

As Director of Field Operations I'm responsible for all aspects of operations relating to the playing surface and surrounding grounds at AutoZone Park. In my six seasons with the organization I have managed baseball and soccer games, field conversions, and outside events like the St. Jude Marathon. I have developed capital and seasonal budgets, hired and scheduled staff, created plans and procedures to streamline operations like field conversions, overseen complete field renovations, curated the agronomic plan for the park, and personally designed and implemented stadium improvements such as the Miracle-Gro Rooftop Garden. Often high stress and fast paced, this job has challeged my problem solving skills while allowing me a creative outlet and has greatly strenthened my people and project management abilities.

SERVICE OVER SELF - Memphis,TN

FACILITIES DIRECTOR /

ASST. CONSTRUCTION DIRECTOR

May 2010-March 2014

Service Over Self, or SOS as it's more commonly known, is a non-profit home repair organization that orignally brought me to Memphis as a Construction Manager for the summer of 2010. In those 10 weeks I fell in love with the organization's mission, the neighborhoods we worked in, and Memphis as a whole. I stuck around for SOS's year-long community development intership, after which I became a full-time staff member serving on the construction staff and as Facilities Director for all of SOS's properties. My responsibilities included overseeing ten different construction sites operated by up to 120 seasonal staff and volunteers, training seasonal staff in construction skills and directly mentoring a team of five staff members, creating and executing year-long facility maintenance plans, and performing related administrative duties.

BINGHAMPTON DEVELOPMENT CORPORATION - Memphis,TN

URBAN FARMS INTERN

August 2010-May 2011

As part of my community development internship I was partnered with the Binghampton Development Corporation to assist with their Urban Farms and associated Urban Farms Market. In this role I participated in planting and harvesting crops, spearheaded multiple infrastructure projects including installing an irrigation system, constructed a work area for processing crops, and built raised beds. Outside of the farm, I delivered fresh fruits and vegetables to local restaurants and contributed to maintaining and improving the Urban Farms Market.
